Brand Design Elements

Web Design & Development: I create a visual mockup of what each page of your website will look like first. I will consult you for any tweaks and changes, and after you are satisfied with how it looks, I move onto the development side, using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring it to life, discussing with you what kind of functionality you need as I progress through bringing your vision to life.

Logo Design: Though logo design is considered graphic design, it is an especially important part of your visual brand, thus the reason why I put this down separately. I will first discuss with you any concept ideas you have, and then I will create ten sketches from those ideas. We will discuss which sketch, if any, fit what you are trying to embody with your business, and then I will work on refining that sketch, and present it to you in black and white. We will discuss if any changes or tweaks need to be made, and add brand colours. Final discussion will lead to handing over vector versions of the logo to be used on whatever branding pieces you need.

Graphic Design: letterhead, vehicle wrap, branded shirts, flyers, brochures, posters, postcards... anything that brand colours, logo, and typography are used on for promotions are offered.

Photography: product photography can be used to promote your products and services, along with the graphic design pieces mentioned above. I also offer photography as a courtesy to pet owners.
